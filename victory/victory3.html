About Me

Innovative Fashion Designer with hands-on expertise in garment construction, trend forecasting, and collaborative design processes. Adept at transforming creative concepts into market-ready collections while streamlining production workflows.

Combines technical proficiency in embroidery and illustration with strong organizational skills to deliver projects on time. Passionate about merging contemporary aesthetics with cultural influences to create impactful, wearable art.

My design philosophy centers on the intersection of tradition and innovation, drawing from my Nigerian heritage while embracing modern techniques and sustainable practices. Each creation tells a story of cultural richness translated through the language of contemporary fashion.
